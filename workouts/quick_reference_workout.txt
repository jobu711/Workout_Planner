================================================================
          QUICK REFERENCE WORKOUT
          11 Exercises | Full Body
================================================================

Equipment: Kettlebell, Dumbbells, Bench
Est. Time: 45-55 minutes


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  WARM-UP
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  1. SLINGSHOT
     20 reps each direction
     Targets: Shoulders, Core, Grip
     Pass KB around body at waist height, switching
     hands front and behind. Core tight, hips stable.

  2. GOOD MORNING
     10 reps
     Targets: Glutes, Hamstrings
     Hold KB at chest or behind head. Hinge at hips,
     push butt back, keep back flat. Stretch hamstrings.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  BLOCK A: KETTLEBELL COMPOUND
  Rest 60-90 sec between sets
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  3. TWO HANDED SWING
     3 sets x 15-20 reps
     Targets: Glutes, Hamstrings, Lower Back, Core
     Hinge at hips, swing KB between legs, explosively
     drive hips forward. Arms stay relaxed.
     Goal: Build to 10 minutes non-stop.

  4. GOBLET SQUAT
     3 sets x 12-15 reps
     Targets: Quads, Glutes, Core
     Hold KB by horns at chest. Squat deep, chest up,
     elbows inside knees. Drive through heels.

  5. SUMO HIGH PULL
     3 sets x 10-12 reps
     Targets: Glutes, Hamstrings, Quads, Traps, Shoulders
     Wide stance, toes out. KB between feet. Drive through
     legs, pull KB to chest height with elbows high.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  BLOCK B: UPPER BODY
  Rest 60-90 sec between sets
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  6. SINGLE ARM ROW
     3 sets x 10-12 each arm
     Targets: Back, Shoulders, Biceps, Hamstrings
     Hinge forward, support on bench or knee. Row KB to
     hip, drive elbow back. Squeeze shoulder blade at top.

  7. CLOSE GRIP DUMBBELL PRESS
     3-4 sets x 8-12 reps
     Targets: Triceps, Chest
     Lie flat, dumbbells together over chest. Press up
     keeping dumbbells close. Triceps focus.

  8. DUMBBELL LYING EXTENSION
     3 sets x 10-12 reps
     Targets: Triceps
     Lie on bench with dumbbells. Lower to sides of head,
     extend back up. Individual arm work.

  9. DUMBBELL KICKBACK
     3 sets x 12-15 each arm
     Targets: Triceps (Lateral Head)
     Bent over, upper arm parallel to floor. Extend
     forearm back until straight. Squeeze at top.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  BLOCK C: CORE FINISHER
  Rest 45-60 sec between sets
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 10. LYING LEG RAISE
     3 sets x 12-15 reps
     Targets: Lower Abs, Hip Flexors
     Lie flat, hands under hips. Legs straight, raise to
     90 degrees. Lower slowly without touching floor.

 11. RUSSIAN TWIST
     2 sets x 20 total
     Targets: Obliques, Core
     V-sit, feet elevated. Hold KB at chest, rotate torso
     side to side. Control the movement.


================================================================
  TOTAL WORKING SETS: ~30
================================================================

  NOTES:
  - Warm up thoroughly before Block A
  - Increase weight when top-end reps feel easy
  - Superset exercises within blocks to save time
  - Track weights in your Workout Planner app

================================================================
