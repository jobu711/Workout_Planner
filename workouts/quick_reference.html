Quick Reference

11 Selected Exercises

Triceps

Dumbbell Kickback

Bent over, upper arm parallel to floor. Extend forearm back until arm is straight. Squeeze at top.

3 sets x 12-15 each arm Guide

Targets: Triceps (Lateral Head)

Close Grip Dumbbell Press

Lie flat, dumbbells together over chest. Press up keeping dumbbells close. Triceps focus with chest involvement.

3-4 sets x 8-12 reps Guide

Targets: Triceps, Chest

Dumbbell Lying Extension

Lie on bench with dumbbells, lower to sides of head, extend back up. Allows individual arm work.

3 sets x 10-12 reps Guide

Targets: Triceps

Kettlebell

Slingshot Warm-Up

Pass the kettlebell around your body at waist height, switching hands in front and behind. Keep your core tight and hips stable.

20 reps each direction

Targets: Shoulders, Core, Grip

Good Morning Warm-Up

Hold KB at chest or behind head. Hinge at hips, pushing butt back while keeping back flat. Feel the stretch in hamstrings.

10 reps

Targets: Glutes, Hamstrings

Two Handed Swing

The foundational KB movement. Hinge at hips, swing KB between legs, then explosively drive hips forward. Arms stay relaxed.

3 sets x 15-20 reps Watch

Targets: Glutes, Hamstrings, Lower Back, Core

Goal: 10 minutes non-stop

Goblet Squat

Hold KB by horns at chest. Squat deep with chest up, elbows tracking inside knees. Drive through heels to stand.

3 sets x 12-15 reps

Targets: Quads, Glutes, Core

Sumo High Pull

Wide stance with toes out. Grip KB between feet, drive through legs and pull KB to chest height with elbows high. Lower and repeat.

3 sets x 10-12 reps Guide

Targets: Glutes, Hamstrings, Quads, Traps, Shoulders

Single Arm Row

Hinge forward, support on bench or knee. Row KB to hip, driving elbow back. Squeeze shoulder blade at top.

3 sets x 10-12 each arm

Targets: Shoulders, Biceps, Back, Hamstrings

Core

Lying Leg Raise

Lie flat, hands under hips for support. Keeping legs straight, raise them to 90 degrees. Lower slowly without touching floor.

3 sets x 12-15 reps Guide

Targets: Lower Abs, Hip Flexors

Russian Twist

V-sit position, feet elevated. Hold KB at chest and rotate torso side to side. Control the movement.

2 sets x 20 total

Targets: Obliques, Core
